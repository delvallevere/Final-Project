About Aggies Tune

NC A&T's Premier Student Singing Community

Our Mission

To provide a supportive environment where NC A&T students can explore vocal music, develop their singing abilities, and build confidence through performance and collaboration.

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Aggies Tune started as a small group of music enthusiasts and has grown into one of the most active performing arts clubs on campus. We believe every Aggie has a voice worth sharing.
